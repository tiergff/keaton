### 简介（机翻）

　　来自著名的文化和电影史学家——这是一部重要的传记，是二十多年来的第一部，讲述了这位传奇喜剧演员和电影制作人，他将身体喜剧提升到了艺术的最高境界，其独创性的电影在今天仍然像近一个世纪前吸引观众时一样令人震惊、创新、现代和不可抗拒。

　　“它太棒了——我完全被吸引住了，停不下来，当它结束的时候，我感到非常抱歉。”——Kevin Brownlow

　　詹姆斯·阿吉将巴斯特·基顿命名为“伟大的石头脸”。阿吉写道，“作为早期美国的原型，基顿几乎与林肯并驾齐驱；这部电影让人难以忘怀、英俊、近乎美丽，但也有着不可还原的幽默感。基顿是唯一一位将情感几乎完全排除在作品之外的主要喜剧演员……他将纯粹的身体喜剧推向了最高境界。”

　　梅尔·布鲁克斯:“我的很多勇气都来自基顿。”

　　马丁·斯科塞斯，在拍摄《愤怒的公牛》时受到基顿的影响，斯科塞斯说：“对我来说，唯一对电影中的拳击持正确态度的人，就是巴斯特·基顿。”

　　基顿戴着一顶卷边帽，面无表情地凝视着观众，就像查理·卓别林扮演的流浪汉、哈罗德·劳埃德戴的草帽和眼镜一样容易辨认，再加上W. C. 菲尔兹(W. C. Fields)，这四个人都被认为是喜剧之王——但基顿过去是，现在仍然是，他们被认为是所有人中最伟大的。

　　他标志性的形象和杂技般的才华掩盖了一个事实：在镜头背后，基顿是我们最具天赋的电影人之一。通过19部短片喜剧和12部精彩的长篇电影，他的作品包括《福尔摩斯二世》、《航海家》、《船长二世》、《摄影师》和他的杰作《将号军》。

　　现在，詹姆斯·柯蒂斯（James Curtis）是普雷斯顿·斯图吉斯（Preston Sturges）、W.C.菲尔兹（W.C.Fields）的著名传记作家，给我们最丰富，最全面的生活，迄今为止的传奇演员，特技演员，编剧，导演大师。

**From acclaimed cultural and film historian—a major biography, the first in more than two decades, of the legendary comedian and filmmaker who elevated physical comedy to the highest of arts and whose ingenious films remain as startling, innovative, modern—and irresistible—today as they were when they beguiled audiences almost a century ago.**

**&#8220;It is brilliant—I was totally absorbed, couldn&#8217;t stop reading it and was very sorry when it ended.&#8221;—Kevin Brownlow**  
  
It was&nbsp;James Agee who christened&nbsp;Buster Keaton “The Great Stone Face.” Keaton’s face, Agee wrote, &#8220;ranked almost with Lincoln’s as an early American archetype; it was haunting, handsome, almost beautiful, yet it was also irreducibly funny. Keaton was the only major comedian who kept sentiment almost entirely out of his work and . . . he brought pure physical comedy to its greatest heights.”  
  
Mel Brooks: “A lot of my daring came from Keaton.”  
  
Martin Scorsese, influenced by Keaton’s pictures in the making of _Raging Bull:_ “The only person who had the right attitude about boxing in the movies for me,” Scorsese said, “was Buster Keaton.”  
  
Keaton’s deadpan stare in a porkpie hat was as recognizable as Charlie Chaplin’s tramp and Harold Lloyd’s straw boater and spectacles, and, with W. C. Fields, the four were each considered a comedy king&#8211;but Keaton was, and still is, considered to be the greatest of them all.  
  
His iconic look and acrobatic brilliance obscured the fact that behind the camera Keaton was one of our most gifted filmmakers. Through nineteen short comedies and twelve magnificent features, he distinguished himself with such seminal works as _Sherlock Jr._, _The Navigator_, _Steamboat Bill, Jr._, _The Cameraman_, and his masterpiece, _The General_.  
  
Now James Curtis, admired biographer of Preston Sturges (“definitive”—_Variety_), W. C. Fields (“by far the fullest, fairest and most touching account we have yet had. Or are likely to have”—Richard Schickel, front page of _The New York Times Book Review_), and Spencer Tracy (“monumental; definitive”—_Kirkus Reviews_), gives us the richest, most comprehensive life to date of the legendary actor, stunt artist, screenwriter, director—_master._

## 关于作者

　　詹姆斯·柯蒂斯是《威廉·卡梅隆·门基斯：未来电影的形态》、《斯宾塞·特雷西：传记》和《W.C.菲尔兹：传记》（获得2004年戏剧图书馆协会奖、评审团特别奖）等著作的作者。他住在加州的布雷亚。

JAMES CURTIS&nbsp;is the author of William Cameron Menzies: The Shape of Films to Come, Spencer Tracy: A Biography, and W. C. Fields: A Biography (winner of the 2004 Theatre Library Association Award, Special Jury Prize), among others. He lives in Brea, California.
