### 简介（机翻）

　　在这部挑战文化史流派的作品中，《Slate》的首席影评人将喜剧传奇和著名导演巴斯特·基顿独特的创作天才放在他所处的时代背景中。

　　巴斯特·基顿出生于1895年，与电影业同一年，他以家庭闹剧的童星身份开始了自己的演艺生涯，该闹剧被认为是歌舞杂技中最暴力的。从20岁出头开始，他在长达10年的时间里担任过导演、演员、特技演员、编辑，以及一些有史以来最伟大的无声喜剧的全能策划，包括《福尔摩斯二世》、《将军号》和《摄影师》。

　　即使在他黑暗的中年，作为一个严重抑郁的酒鬼，在演艺行业的边缘寻找工作，他的生活也反映了他周围世界正在发生的变化。他在三种不同媒介的创作巅峰时期取得了成功：首先是杂耍，然后是默片，最后是实验性的早期电视。在他精彩纷飞的70年人生历程中，他的人生轨迹与许多有影响力的人物相交，如脱逃大师哈里·胡迪尼、黑人舞台喜剧先驱伯特·威廉姆斯、电视传奇人物露西尔·鲍尔，以及文学创新者F.斯科特·菲茨杰拉德和塞缪尔·贝克特。

　　在这本书中，影评人达纳·史蒂文斯将镜头从基顿的生活和工作中拉出来，着眼于娱乐、新闻、法律、技术、女性的政治和社会地位以及大众对上瘾的理解的同时发展。凭借渊博的学识和闪烁的幽默，史蒂文斯在各个学科之间跳跃，把我们带到今天，当基顿惊人（有时危及生命）的特技表演以表情包动图的形式在互联网上传播时，比以往任何时候都更受欢迎。这本书远不只是一部传记或一部电影史作品，它是一部关于现代性的广泛思考，描绘了一位独一无二的艺术家的复杂肖像。

**In this genre-defying work of cultural history, the chief film critic of _Slate_ places comedy legend and acclaimed filmmaker Buster Keaton’s unique creative genius in the context of his time.**

Born the same year as the film industry in 1895, Buster Keaton began his career as the child star of a family slapstick act reputed to be the most violent in vaudeville. Beginning in his early twenties, he enjoyed a decade-long stretch as the director, star, stuntman, editor, and all-around mastermind of some of the greatest silent comedies ever made, including _Sherlock Jr_., _The General_, and _The Cameraman_.  
  
Even through his dark middle years as a severely depressed alcoholic finding work on the margins of show business, Keaton’s life had a way of reflecting the changes going on in the world around him. He found success in three different mediums at their creative peak: first vaudeville, then silent film, and finally the experimental early years of television. Over the course of his action-packed seventy years on earth, his life trajectory intersected with those of such influential figures as the escape artist Harry Houdini, the pioneering Black stage comedian Bert Williams, the television legend Lucille Ball, and literary innovators like F. Scott Fitzgerald and Samuel Beckett.  
  
In _Camera Man_, film critic Dana Stevens pulls the lens out from Keaton’s life and work to look at concurrent developments in entertainment, journalism, law, technology, the political and social status of women, and the popular understanding of addiction. With erudition and sparkling humor, Stevens hopscotches among disciplines to bring us up to the present day, when Keaton’s breathtaking (and sometimes life-threatening) stunts remain more popular than ever as they circulate on the internet in the form of viral gifs. Far more than a biography or a work of film history, _Camera Man_ is a wide-ranging meditation on modernity that paints a complex portrait of a one-of-a-kind artist.
